This page is a randomiser for positions in a football match. It is primarily used for FIFA which is an online football game where people can play in the same team together and choose the position they want to play in the game. This page prompts the user to choose a formation and then input a name or number when clicking on 'Random Position' and it will then assign the given name/number to a position in the currently selected formation.
