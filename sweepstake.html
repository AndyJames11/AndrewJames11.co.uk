This page is a simple sweepstake page for the 2024 Euros competition. The page is used to take an input from the user when they click on the 'Sweepstake' button and then assign that given input to one of the countries playing in the Euros competition. The idea of the sweepstake is that, whoever is assigned the country that ends up winning the tournament, is the winner of the sweepstake.
