This page is a randomiser for League of Legends which is a popular PC video game. The page prompts users to click the 'Random Role' button which will then display a role to play within the game. Once the role has been chosen, the user can then click on the role icon below to be given a random champion for that role. The roles from top-left to bottom-right are Top, Jungle, Mid, ADC and Support.
